## 🚀 The Product Development Process
Working with a freelance engineer shouldn't be a black box. Here is how I help you scale:


1.  **Discovery:** We define the constraints, budget, and end-user needs.
2.  **Concept & CAD:** I build the 3D architecture and iterate based on your feedback.
3.  **Prototyping:** We build "looks-like" and "works-like" models to validate the design.
4.  **Refinement:** I apply DFM principles to ensure the product can be made reliably and affordably.
5.  **Hand-off:** You receive all source files and the technical package for your manufacturer.
